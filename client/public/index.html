Rules go here

Each gaming category has three (3) sections in which judges will be judging you on. Each section is worth 100 points. Judges will rate your performance in each section of the category using a 10 point scale system. You will get 100 or a fraction of 100 based on these ratings for each section, up to a total of 300 points per category. If you dominate each category, an additional 50 points will be added to your total. Below is an example of how your scores for Platformer could look like:

Judge H Judge J Judge K
Section 1 10 5 2
Section 2 5 5 5
Section 3 9 10 10

The highest number of points you can get per section is 100. The highest score you can get from all judges is 30 (i.e. H, J, and K give you a 10)

  • For Section 1, your points are (17 / 30) * 100, which equals 57
  • For Section 2, your points are (15 / 30) * 100, which equals 50
  • For Section 3, your points are (29 / 30) * 100, which equals 97

This means that for the entire Platformer category, your final score is: 204

Say, however, that 57, 50, and 97 are the highest scores that each section got amongst all players. That is, you scored the highest on each individual section of the platformer category. You will get an additional 50 points added to your total. Your new final score is: 254
